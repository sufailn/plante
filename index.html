Landscaping

Landscaping is more than just planting—it’s about creating an environment that feels natural and inviting. We design and build landscapes that enhance both residential and commercial spaces, using a mix of greenery, textures, and structural elements. Every landscape we design is tailored to suit the space, style, and functionality you envision.

Irrigation

We offer tailored irrigation solutions, from automated sprinklers to precision drip systems, ensuring efficient hydration with minimal waste. Designed for reliability and ease of maintenance, our systems adapt to any landscape, providing optimal water management for healthy plants.

Hardscaping

Hardscaping adds structure and character to any outdoor space. At Plante, we design and install durable, aesthetic elements like patios, walkways, retaining walls, and decorative stonework. With the right materials and layout, we create spaces that are both functional and visually appealing. Our installations are built to last, ensuring both practicality and long-term durability. With a focus on sustainability, we use eco-friendly materials that complement the natural surroundings.

Moss Walls

Moss walls bring nature indoors with a lush, low-maintenance design. At Plante, we create custom moss walls that enhance spaces with texture, color, and a calming ambiance. Requiring no watering or sunlight, our eco-friendly moss walls improve air quality, reduce noise, and add a natural touch to any setting. Stylish and sustainable, they seamlessly blend beauty with functionality.
