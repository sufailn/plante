Hardscaping

Hardscaping adds structure and character to any outdoor space. At Plante, we design and install durable, aesthetic elements like patios, walkways, retaining walls, and decorative stonework. With the right materials and layout, we create spaces that are both functional and visually appealing. Our installations are built to last, ensuring both practicality and long-term durability. With a focus on sustainability, we use eco-friendly materials that complement the natural surroundings.

Irrigation

We offer tailored irrigation solutions, from automated sprinklers to precision drip systems, ensuring efficient hydration with minimal waste. Designed for reliability and ease of maintenance, our systems adapt to your landscape’s needs, making plant care effortless. Whether for a garden, commercial space, or indoor greenery, our irrigation solutions keep your plants healthy with optimal water management. Each solution is customized to fit the specific needs of your landscape, ensuring proper hydration without overwatering. With automated and low-maintenance options, plant care becomes effortless and efficient.

Landscaping

Landscaping is more than just planting—it’s about creating an environment that feels natural and inviting. We design and build landscapes that enhance both residential and commercial spaces, using a mix of greenery, textures, and structural elements. Every landscape we design is tailored to suit the space, style, and functionality you envision. We integrate sustainable practices, using plants that thrive with minimal upkeep while enhancing the overall aesthetics. Whether it's a lush garden or a minimalist outdoor retreat, our landscapes bring a perfect balance of beauty and purpose.
